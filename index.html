We are a team of two hard-workers that create simple and beautiful digital experiences and resources with the intention of organizing and curating some of the world's most popular online topics. We created Quarry out of a need we saw for curated resources for people from all knowledge and skill levels.

As graphic designers, we both understand the value of providing experiences and resources that are designed beautifully, curated carefully, communicate quickly, and work efficiently. The realm of online shopping and reference has grown to unimaginable proportions. Quarry provides consumers and creatives the knowledge base they need to make informed purchasing decisions through our ecommerce sites as well as use the tools we create to help people work and grow confidently.
